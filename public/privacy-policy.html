Your privacy matters: This privacy policy explains how UK Government Services collects, uses, and protects your personal information in compliance with UK data protection laws.

1. Who we are

Data Controller: UK Government Services Digital Team

Contact: privacy@ukgovservices.gov.uk

Address: UK Government Services, Digital Services Team, London, UK

Data Protection Officer: dpo@ukgovservices.gov.uk

2. What personal information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

Data Type Examples Purpose Legal Basis
Contact Information Name, email address, phone number, postal address Service delivery, communication Legitimate interest / Consent
Technical Information IP address, browser type, device information Website functionality, security Legitimate interest
Usage Information Pages visited, time spent, search queries Service improvement, analytics Legitimate interest

3. How we collect your information

We collect personal information through:

  • Direct provision: When you use our services, contact us, or provide information
  • Automated collection: Through cookies, analytics, and website interactions
  • Third parties: From other government departments (where legally permitted)
  • Public sources: Publicly available government databases and records

4. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide government services and information
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct research and analysis for public benefit
  • Communicate important updates about our services

5. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Public task: Providing government services and information
  • Legitimate interests: Website functionality, security, and improvement
  • Consent: Optional services and communications (where applicable)
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with statutory requirements

6. Who we share your information with

We may share your personal information with:

  • Other government departments: For service delivery and legal compliance
  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our services
  • Law enforcement: When required by law or to prevent crime
  • Legal advisors: For legal advice and compliance

Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

7. International transfers

Your personal data is primarily processed within the UK. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure:

  • Adequate protection through UK adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • Compliance with UK data protection requirements

8. How long we keep your information

We retain personal information for different periods depending on:

  • Service data: As long as necessary to provide the service
  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months
  • Contact records: Up to 7 years for government accountability
  • Legal requirements: As required by applicable laws

10. Security measures

We protect your personal information through:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Compliance with government security standards
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

11. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies. For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

12. Children's privacy

Our services are not specifically designed for children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

13. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "last updated" date
  • Notify you of significant changes where required by law
